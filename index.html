Dear Applicant,

On behalf of the Admissions Committee, it is my pleasure to offer you admission to the MIT Class of 2029. You were identified as one of the most talented and promising students in one of MIT's most competitive applicant pools ever. Your commitment to personal excellence makes you stand out as someone who will thrive within our academic environment as well as contribute to our diverse community.

This year, we received over 33,767 applications for admission. The Committee was impressed by your academic achievements, your passion for discovery, and your potential to make meaningful contributions to our community and the world. Your application demonstrated not only exceptional ability but also the creativity, collaborative spirit, and drive that characterize MIT students.

At MIT, you join kindred spirits: scholars, builders, entrepreneurs, and humanitarians. We believe that you and MIT are very well matched for each other. While you may have offers from many fine schools, we hope that you'll choose to enroll at MIT.

The deadline to accept our offer is May 1, 2025. Between now and then, we look forward to building our relationship with you so you can get to know us better. We'll be in touch via email and through your myMIT portal account.

I hope this letter brings you the joy you deserve to feel. Please take time to celebrate with your loved ones. But remember, this offer of admission is contingent upon your successful completion of the school year with continued strong performance.

Finally, I hope you'll agree with us that MIT is the perfect place to prepare you for your future role in a world that badly needs you.

Congratulations and welcome to the MIT Class of 2029. I look forward to seeing you on campus.

Stuart Schmill
Dean of Admissions and Student Financial Services
